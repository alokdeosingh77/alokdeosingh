About

Turning strategy into measurable outcomes

I'm a Senior Program Manager currently leading product modernization and AI transformation at Brightly (a Siemens Company) in Noida. Over two decades, I've navigated complex programs — migrating monolith platforms to microservices SaaS, building ML-powered recommendation engines, securing IoT fleets, and launching insurance products across geographies.

I believe the best programs aren't just delivered — they're designed to last. That means obsessing over KPIs, building governance frameworks that surface risk early, and partnering with engineering, product, and executive stakeholders to keep delivery aligned with strategy.

Recently completed my Chief Digital & AI Officer (CDAIO) program at ISB Hyderabad. I'm passionate about how AI and data platforms are reshaping enterprise operations.
